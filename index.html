Our Story

A Foundation Built on the Love of Music

Hawa Music Foundation was established with a singular vision: to make music education accessible, joyful, and transformative. We celebrate the rich heritage of Eastern music while embracing Western traditions, creating a unique space where cultures harmonize.

From classical instruments to modern performance, our programs are designed to inspire students of all ages and skill levels — from their very first note to professional mastery.

Belal Alhaj Younes
Founder & Director
